# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Close family and friends of Tin Vo and Yen Nguyen — many traveling from Vietnam and the Vietnamese diaspora in the US. They open the link on phones at home, share it with relatives, return to watch the countdown tick down. They want to feel the weight of the occasion, understand logistics clearly, and show the site to others with pride.

## Product Purpose

A digital wedding invitation grounded in the specific place and story of Tin and Yen's love — Cà Mau, Vietnam's southernmost land, where the Mekong meets the sea. Success is guests feeling genuinely moved on first open, and returning before November 28, 2026.

## Brand Personality

Intimate, luminous, rooted. The feeling of candlelight on water at night — still, warm, quietly overwhelming.

## Anti-references

- Generic luxury wedding templates (Zola, The Knot, Squarespace wedding themes)
- Busy maximalism: oversaturated gold gradients, baroque ornament piled on baroque ornament
- Corporate dark-luxury: SaaS hero sections dressed in black and gold
- Editorial-magazine lane: display serif + italic drop caps + broadsheet grid — this is a wedding invitation, not Kinfolk
- Tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows above every section

## Design Principles

1. **Earned stillness** — motion creates meaning, not decoration. Each transition should feel like a held breath.
2. **The geography is the story** — Cà Mau, the Mekong, the mangroves, the sea — this specific place shapes the visual world.
3. **Let the names breathe** — every screen hierarchy leads back to Tin and Yen. Typography serves them, never competes.
4. **Vietnamese in spirit, global in craft** — at home on Awwwards AND recognizable to a grandmother in Cà Mau.
5. **Invite wonder, not overwhelm** — guests should feel held, not impressed-at. Intimacy at every scale.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Bilingual EN/VI: preserve and honor
- Mobile-first: guests in Vietnam are primarily on mobile
- Reduced motion: respect prefers-reduced-motion throughout
- WCAG AA minimum for all text contrast
